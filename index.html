About This Project

Open Frederick makes public data about Frederick County, Virginia accessible and understandable. We collect, process, and visualize data from official government sources to help residents, researchers, and policymakers understand how the county operates.

All data is sourced from official public records including county budget documents, Virginia Department of Education reports, Commissioner of Revenue tax records, and federal databases. Every data point includes source citations for verification.
