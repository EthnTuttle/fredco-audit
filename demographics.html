How This Analysis Was Conducted

This demographic analysis was performed by classifying property owner surnames using a local large language model (LLM). Here's the methodology:

  • Data Source: Frederick County Real Estate Tax Book (2025) containing 48,532 property records
  • Filtering: Business entities (LLCs, trusts, corporations) were excluded, leaving 35,764 individual property owners
  • Classification: Each unique surname (11,695 total) was classified by the llama3.2:3b model into ethnic/cultural origin categories
  • Categories: Anglo-American, Hispanic/Latino, Asian, Middle Eastern/North African (MENA), Other European, Unknown
  • Prompt: The model was asked to classify surnames based on typical ethnic/cultural associations (e.g., Smith = Anglo, Garcia = Hispanic, Nguyen = Asian)

Important Limitations: This analysis classifies surnames, not individuals. A surname does not determine ethnicity, citizenship, or identity. Many Americans have surnames from various origins due to marriage, adoption, immigration history, and cultural heritage. This data shows surname patterns only and should not be used to make assumptions about individuals.
